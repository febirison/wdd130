Rafting Site Plan

SPLASH

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to transform Splash into the go-to destination for unforgettable whitewater rafting adventures in Ghana. We'll achieve this by showcasing the thrill of the rapids, providing clear trip information, and offering a seamless booking experience. Our website will also build a community around the excitement of rafting and solidify our reputation as a reliable and experienced rafting company.

Audience

We target adventure seekers, families, and nature lovers looking for an unforgettable whitewater rafting experience in Ghana.

Typography

Montserrat

Sans-serif

Montserrat is a modern sans-serif font with a strong presence. It's well-suited for headings due to its high legibility and bold appearance.

Open Sans

Open Sans is a clean and readable sans-serif font that works well for body text and callout boxes. It provides good contrast with Montserrat headings while maintaining a modern and approachable feel.
